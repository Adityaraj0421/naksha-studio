## Caption

**Full caption copy (150 words):**

We cut onboarding time by 40% — here's exactly what we changed 🧵

67% of SaaS users never complete onboarding. We had the data. Our own numbers were telling the same story.

So we went back to basics: 18 user interviews, 6 weeks of testing, and one guiding question — what's the fastest path to the first 'aha' moment?

The answer wasn't a new UI. It was radical subtraction.

9 steps became 3. Every screen earned its place. We built toward activation, not completion.

Result: 40% faster time-to-value, 28% lift in activation, 4.6/5 satisfaction score.

The biggest lesson? Users don't need more guidance. They need less friction.

Full teardown is linked in bio — includes the wireframes, the research brief, and what we'd do differently.

What's your biggest onboarding challenge? Drop it below — we're building a follow-up post from your answers.
