# /social-campaign — Lumina SaaS AI Analytics Feature Launch

**Input:** `multi-platform launch campaign — Lumina AI analytics feature, 3 weeks, LinkedIn + Twitter/X + Instagram`
**Goal:** Drive 500 trial signups and 2,000 video views from SaaS founders and product leaders

---

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign name:** "See the Future of Your SaaS"
**Duration:** 3 weeks (21 days)
**Launch date:** 2026-04-07
**Platforms:** LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram
**Total budget allocation:** Distributed across three platforms (see Platform Strategy)
**Primary objective:** 500 trial signups via social-attributed UTM tracking
**Secondary objective:** 50 demo requests, 2,000 video views, 15% email list growth

**Campaign premise:**
Lumina's new AI analytics feature predicts churn, surfaces growth opportunities, and surfaces revenue signals before they appear in standard dashboards. The campaign positions this not as a feature launch but as a capability shift — from reactive reporting to proactive intelligence.

**Core message:**
> "Your SaaS data already knows what's coming. Lumina helps you see it first."

---

## Platform Strategy

**LinkedIn — 40% of budget**
- Role: Thought leadership + sponsored content
- Format mix: Long-form posts (800–1,200 words), document carousels, sponsored single-image ads
- Audience: SaaS founders, heads of product, VPs of growth at 10–200 person companies
- Tone: Analytical, credibility-forward — data, case studies, expert framing
- Organic cadence: 5 posts over 3 weeks
- Paid: 2 sponsored post boosts (week 2 feature reveal + week 3 social proof)

**Twitter/X — 30% of budget**
- Role: Product demos + community threads
- Format mix: Product demo GIFs, thread breakdowns, reply engagement, promoted tweets
- Audience: Builders and operators following SaaS-adjacent accounts; tech-forward product community
- Tone: Direct, fast, founder-to-founder
- Organic cadence: 2–3 tweets or threads per week
- Paid: 1 promoted video tweet (60s demo, week 2)

**Instagram — 30% of budget**
- Role: Visual storytelling + brand awareness
- Format mix: Carousels, Reels (30s), Stories with polls and CTAs
- Audience: Designers, PMs, early-stage founders — aspirational and visual
- Tone: Clean aesthetic, product-forward, light on jargon
- Organic cadence: 2 carousels + 3 Reels over 3 weeks
- Paid: 1 boosted Reel (product demo, week 2)

**platform budget rationale:**
LinkedIn receives the largest share because it delivers the highest-intent B2B audience and the lowest cost-per-trial-signup historically for Lumina. Twitter/X supports community seeding and amplification. Instagram builds visual brand equity and reaches a secondary audience of early-adopter founders.

---

## Audience Targeting

**Primary audience:**
- SaaS founders and product leaders
- Company size: 10–200 employees
- Stage: Post-product-market-fit, scaling
- Geography: US, UK, Canada, Australia (English-primary markets)
- Job titles: Founder, CEO, Head of Product, VP Product, Chief of Staff, Growth Lead

**Lookalike audience:**
- Seed: Existing Lumina Pro customers (export from CRM — minimum 500-person seed list)
- Platform: LinkedIn Matched Audiences + Meta Custom Audiences
- Expansion ratio: 2× lookalike on LinkedIn, 3× on Instagram

**Retargeting segments:**
- Website visitors (last 30 days, non-converting)
- Trial users who did not activate (email suppression list excluded from paid)
- Video viewers (50%+ watch time) who did not click CTA

**Exclusions:**
- Existing paid customers (suppress from all paid campaigns)
- Competitors (keyword exclusion list: 15 competitor brand names)
- Irrelevant verticals: e-commerce, hospitality, retail

**audience note:**
The lookalike audience off Lumina Pro customers consistently outperforms cold interest-based targeting by 2.3× on trial signup rate. Prioritize this segment in week 2 paid activation.

## Creative Brief

**Hero asset — 60s product demo video**
- Purpose: Core paid creative, used across all three platforms (platform-resized)
- Format: 16:9 master → 1:1 (Instagram/LinkedIn) → 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
- Structure: 0–5s hook (problem stat), 5–45s demo (screen recording with voiceover), 45–60s CTA
- Hook frame: "Most SaaS teams find out about churn 30 days too late." — white text on dark brand background
- Voiceover: Founder voice, conversational — not polished ad narrator
- Captions: Burned-in subtitles required (85% of video views are silent)
- CTA end card: "Start your free trial — lumina.io/trial"

**Supporting creatives:**

| Asset | Qty | Platform | Week |
|-------|-----|----------|------|
| LinkedIn carousel (feature breakdown) | 3 | LinkedIn | 1, 2, 3 |
| Twitter/X demo GIF | 2 | Twitter/X | 1, 2 |
| Instagram carousel (visual storytelling) | 3 | Instagram | 1, 2, 3 |
| Social proof quote cards | 5 | All | 2, 3 |
| Blog post (supporting SEO + LinkedIn share) | 1 | LinkedIn | 2 |
| Stories sequence (poll + swipe-up) | 3 | Instagram | 1, 2, 3 |

**creative direction:**
All creative assets must use the Lumina brand system: primary gradient (#5C6EFF → #A78BFA), neutral dark background (#0F0F1A), white body copy, and Lumina Display typeface for headlines. Product screenshots must use the latest staging environment. Do not use stock photography — use product UI, data visualizations, and real customer quotes only.

## Campaign Objectives

**Primary objectives:**

| KPI | Target | Measurement |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| Trial signups | 500 | UTM `utm_campaign=ai-analytics-launch` |
| Demo requests | 50 | CRM form submission, same UTM |
| Video views (50%+) | 2,000 | Platform native analytics |
| Email list growth | +15% | Mailchimp subscriber delta |

**Secondary objectives:**

| KPI | Target |
|-----|--------|
| LinkedIn impressions | 80,000 |
| Instagram Reel views | 15,000 |
| Twitter/X impressions | 25,000 |
| Paid ROAS | ≥ 3× (LTV-weighted) |

**objective tracking setup:**
- UTM parameters required on every link: `utm_source`, `utm_medium`, `utm_campaign`, `utm_content`
- Google Analytics 4 goal configured for trial signup confirmation page
- LinkedIn Insight Tag installed on lumina.io for conversion tracking
- Meta Pixel installed and events verified: PageView, Lead, StartTrial
- Weekly performance review every Monday — pause underperforming ad sets at < 1% CTR after 3 days

**success definition:**
Campaign is considered successful if 400+ trial signups are achieved (80% of target) with ROAS ≥ 2.5×. Stretch goal is 600 signups and 75 demo requests. If Twitter/X paid CPL exceeds 2× LinkedIn CPL by end of week 2, reallocate budget to LinkedIn.
