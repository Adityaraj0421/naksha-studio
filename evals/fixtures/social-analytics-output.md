## Insights & Recommendations

**Key insights from Q1 2026:**

**insight 1 — Carousel posts are the highest-ROI content format**
Carousel posts on both LinkedIn and Instagram consistently outperform all other formats (3.2× vs. single images). Every quarter's top-performing posts have included at least one carousel. Q2 should increase carousel production from 8 to 12 (LinkedIn) and from 3 to 5 (Instagram).

**insight 2 — Founder-face content doubles engagement**
The 3 posts featuring the Lumina founder achieved 8.1% average engagement — the highest format rate in Q1 and 2× the non-founder video rate. This is a high-leverage, low-cost content type. Recommend committing to 1 founder-face post per week in Q2.

**insight 3 — Twitter/X paid spend is underperforming**
Twitter/X's blended CPL of $8.90 is 2.1× LinkedIn's CPL of $4.20 and its engagement rate (2.8%) is the only platform below benchmark. The organic community value remains — educational threads and product demos perform well organically. Recommendation: pause all Twitter/X paid spend in Q2 and reallocate budget to LinkedIn and Instagram. Monitor organic performance; only reinstate paid if CPL drops below $6.00.

**insight 4 — Instagram Reels punch above their weight**
Despite Instagram representing only 17% of total impressions, it drove 27.9% of trial signups — a significantly higher conversion rate per impression than the platform's share would suggest. Q2 should increase Reels output from 5 to 8, with a focus on 30-second product demos.
